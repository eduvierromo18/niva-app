# Mission

Niva helps users understand, organize, and operate their personal finances from a calm, reliable workspace.

The product must turn scattered financial information into decisions the user can trust:

- What is available.
- What changed.
- What needs attention.
- What can wait.
- What action should happen next.

## Product Promise

Niva gives the user a clear financial present and a practical path forward.
