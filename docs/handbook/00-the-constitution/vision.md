# Vision

Niva is a personal financial control center.

It helps the user answer three questions quickly:

1. How much money do I have?
2. How am I doing this month?
3. Where is my money going?

Niva should help people understand their present, make better decisions, and sustain healthy financial habits.

## Product Direction

- Clear before complex.
- Trustworthy before opaque automation.
- Daily usefulness before isolated features.
- Incremental evolution guided by Product Specs.

## Inspiration

- Wallet for speed.
- Copilot and Monarch for financial insights.
- Linear and Vercel for clarity and product quality.

These references define quality direction. They must not be copied as interfaces.
