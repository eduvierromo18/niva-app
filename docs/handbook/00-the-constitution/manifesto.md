# Manifesto

Niva exists to make personal finance feel clear, calm, and workable.

Personal money is often fragmented across accounts, cards, payments, goals, and obligations. Niva brings that reality into one space where the user can understand the present, decide what matters, and act without noise.

Niva is not a spreadsheet replacement, a banking clone, or a dashboard made for decoration. It is a product for daily financial orientation.

The product should feel professional, human, and trustworthy. It should explain money without drama. It should make progress visible without exaggeration. It should respect the fact that financial clarity is emotional as much as numerical.

The Handbook is the source of truth for that direction.
