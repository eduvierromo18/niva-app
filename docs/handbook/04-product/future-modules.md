# Future Modules

## Overview

Future modules collect product ideas that are not ready for implementation.

## Purpose

Keep future direction visible without treating ideas as committed scope.

## User Goals

- Understand possible product expansion.
- Keep roadmap conversations organized.
- Avoid mixing ideas with approved specs.

## Primary Question

What could Niva become later?

## Information Hierarchy

1. Candidate module.
2. User problem.
3. Potential value.
4. Dependencies.
5. Open questions.

## Components

- Product Spec template.
- ADR template.
- Roadmap backlog.

## Interactions

- Promote an idea into a Product Spec.
- Archive ideas that no longer fit the vision.

## States

- Candidate.
- Researching.
- Spec needed.
- Deferred.

## Future Ideas

- Command palette.
- Financial health.
- Widgets.
- Mobile apps.
- Landing.
