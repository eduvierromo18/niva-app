# Accounts

## Overview

Accounts organizes where the user's money lives.

## Purpose

Help the user understand balances by account and institution.

## User Goals

- See account balances.
- Compare account types.
- Add or update accounts.
- Recognize banks quickly.

## Primary Question

Where is my money?

## Information Hierarchy

1. Total available across accounts.
2. Account cards.
3. Bank identity and account type.
4. Status and actions.

## Components

- AccountCard.
- BankAvatar.
- BankSelect.
- AccountSelect.
- CurrencyAmount.
- EmptyState.

## Interactions

- Create account.
- Edit account.
- Select account in financial flows.

## States

- Active accounts.
- Empty account list.
- Archived or inactive accounts.
- Loading state.

## Future Ideas

- Account groups.
- Balance history.
- Institution-level insights.
