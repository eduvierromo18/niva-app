# Home

## Overview

Home gives the user a fast, calm, actionable reading of their daily financial state.

## Purpose

Help the user understand what they can pay, what needs review, and what action is useful without entering detailed modules first.

## User Goals

- Understand available money.
- See monthly movement.
- Review accounts and upcoming payments.
- Notice recent activity and meaningful insights.

## Primary Question

How is my money today?

## Information Hierarchy

1. Main financial state.
2. Income, expenses, balance, and savings.
3. Accounts and upcoming payments.
4. Recent activity.
5. Insights and goals.

## Components

- Hero with "Tu dinero".
- Focus Card.
- KPI cards.
- Quick actions.
- Accounts summary.
- Upcoming payments.
- Recent activity.
- Insights.
- Goals.

## Interactions

- Open existing new account action.
- Open existing new movement action.
- Navigate to module detail when needed.

## States

- Normal financial summary.
- Empty or low-data states.
- Alert states for payments or negative trends.

## Future Ideas

- More contextual insights.
- Better upcoming scheduled transaction preview.
- Personalized focus card.
