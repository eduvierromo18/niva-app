# Voice

## Purpose

Define how Niva speaks to the user.

## Source

This chapter reuses `docs/VOICE_AND_TONE.md`.

## Voice

Clear, human, sober, and reliable.

## Tone

- Direct for actions.
- Calm for financial states.
- Precise for data.
- Motivating without exaggeration.

## Writing Rules

- Prefer plain language.
- Avoid pressure-driven copy.
- Use exact financial terms when precision matters.
- Make next actions obvious.
