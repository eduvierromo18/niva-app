# Motion

## Purpose

Define motion principles for Niva.

## Status

Alpha

## Scope

Applies to transitions, overlays, feedback, loading states, state changes, and microinteractions.

## Principles

- Motion should orient, not decorate.
- Transitions should reinforce continuity between states.
- Animation should be brief, sober, and consistent.
- The experience must remain usable without relying on motion.
